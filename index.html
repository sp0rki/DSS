Born in the heart of Sydney's west, Midnight Vibes is the electrifying solo project of multi-instrumentalist and producer Alex Vane. Blending dark R&B atmospherics with neo-soul grooves and indie pop hooks, their sound has redefined Australia's contemporary music landscape over the past five years.

With four studio albums under their belt — including the double-platinum After Midnight and the critically acclaimed Neon Silence — Vane has cemented a reputation for visceral live performances that leave audiences breathless.

Everything you need to know about Qudos Bank Arena to make your night seamless from arrival to departure.

Address & Directions

Qudos Bank Arena
Olympic Blvd, Sydney Olympic Park NSW 2127

  • By Train: Olympic Park Station (T3 line, alight at Olympic Park)
  • By Bus: Routes 526, 525 from Parramatta
  • By Car: Follow Olympic Park signage from M4 or Parramatta Road
  • Walk: 5 min from Olympic Park Station

Parking

Multiple car parks available across Sydney Olympic Park precinct. Pre-booking strongly recommended — event nights sell out early. Visit sydneyolympicpark.com.au for current availability and rates.

Accessibility

Wheelchair Access Companion Tickets Hearing Loop Accessible Toilets

Contact us for specific accessibility requirements. Companion tickets available at no charge with proof of disability.

Entry Rules & Prohibited Items

  • Bags must not exceed 30cm × 20cm × 10cm
  • No professional cameras or recording equipment
  • No outside food or beverages
  • No glass containers of any kind
  • No umbrellas or laser pointers
  • No weapons or dangerous items
  • Valid photo ID required for licensed areas (18+)
  • Re-entry is not permitted once you have exited

At the Venue

  • Food and beverage available throughout the venue
  • Official merch stands open from doors
  • First aid stations at every concourse level
  • Lost property collected at main information desk
  • ATMs available at concourse level 2
